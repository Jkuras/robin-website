Fill my heart with song
Let me sing forever more..

In other words… singing is a passion for Robin Dalynn. The seed for a garden of music was planted when she became a song leader for Outdoor School campfires as well as her own elementary classroom. Her repertoire includes jazz, folk, country, pop and even children’s music. Her work as a vocalist began in 1985, when a friend encouraged her to finally sing in public. She played guitar in a duo; acoustic folk music with an eclectic blend which proved to be just the beginning of her musical journey. Duos, trios, quartets, jazz choirs and even solo work filled her musical career. For over 20 years, Robin shared family concerts with her husband on banjo, mandolin, and washboard, as the “Rich & Robin” show. Sometimes they were lucky enough to have their 2 boys, Josh & Jesse, join in the band! Her latest love is the American Songbook, sharing the history of jazz, by singing some of the most uplifting, heartwarming, memorable lyrics ever written from a depression era gone by.

At last....

With her son Jesse on bass, her first opportunity to do a professional recording was in the Robert Lang Studio of Seattle, WA. A full band rounds out the sound with smiling Dave Hoskins on drums, the ever extraordinaire Ken Kassover on piano, and our recording engineer in charge, Joel Williams on guitar. Jesse’s band, the Neptune Quartet, is just one of the many configurations she currently works with as well as Trio of Three, Lucky Avalon, and The Take 7 Little Big Band. She even continues to share kid’s classics with her husband Rich. She especially loves working at big events like weddings, anniversary celebrations, and special parties that request unique songs. Being a part of someone’s extraordinary day makes that special song that much sweeter.

So bring Robin Dalynn to your next event and let the music fill your heart with song!!
